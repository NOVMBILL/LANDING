PGP quickstart (for you)

  1. Locate your key: gpg --list-keys
  2. Export ASCII public key: gpg --export -a "Your Name" > publickey.asc
  3. Show/record fingerprint: gpg --fingerprint "Your Name"
  4. Upload publickey.asc to /pgp/publickey.asc on your site.
  5. Paste fingerprint above and keep it consistent everywhere.

Do not upload your key as a PDF. Use plain .asc text.
